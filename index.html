Koch Snowflake

This fractal (sometimes called the Koch curve) is created by taking each line seqment, dividing it into three equal parts, then inserting an equilateral triangle into the middle section. It is one of the earliest fractal shapes to have be explored. It is named after Swedish mathematician Helge von Koch
